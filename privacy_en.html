Privacy Policy for FamLedger

Last Updated: April 24, 2026

Your privacy is fundamental to us. This policy explains how FamLedger handles your information, including the new wealth management and PAC (Dollar Cost Averaging) features.

1. Data Collection and Asset Management

FamLedger is an "offline-first" application. All financial data entered (transactions, account balances, investment details, average cost price, accrued interest, savings goals) is saved exclusively on your device in a secure local database.

We do not have access to your financial data, we do not upload it to external servers, and we do not sell it to third parties. Complex calculations (Net Worth, compound interest on fixed-income accounts) occur entirely locally.

2. Market Data and Third-Party APIs

To provide real-time quotes for stocks, ETFs, and cryptocurrencies, the app can retrieve market data from third-party providers to update investment prices. The app only sends symbol codes (Tickers) to retrieve market prices. No personal data or information about the amount of assets owned is ever sent to these providers.

3. Advertising and Consent

The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements in the free version. AdMob may collect device identifiers to personalize ads. For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we use Google's UMP SDK to allow you to manage your consent in compliance with GDPR.

4. PRO Version and In-App Purchases

Purchases to unlock the PRO version (which removes ads and enables advanced features like Investment Statistics and Anomalies) are handled securely through the Google Play Store. FamLedger does not collect or store payment data or credit card numbers.

5. Manual Backup

The Backup function generates a file (JSON or CSV) containing your data. You decide if and where to export this file (e.g., Google Drive, Email). FamLedger does not perform automatic transfers or cloud synchronization without your manual intervention.

6. Contact Us

For any questions regarding your data management, you can contact us at: famledger.app@gmail.com
