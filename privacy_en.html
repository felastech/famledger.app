Privacy Policy for FamLedger

Last Updated: April 9, 2026

Your privacy is fundamental to us. This policy explains how FamLedger handles your information.

1. Data Collection

FamLedger is an "offline-first" application. All financial data entered (transactions, accounts, categories, goals) is saved exclusively on your device in a local encrypted database.

We do not have access to your financial data, we do not upload it to external servers, and we do not sell it to third parties.

2. Advertising and Third-Party Services

The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers (IDFA for iOS, AAID for Android) to personalize ads and analyze their effectiveness.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), the app displays a consent form (UMP SDK) to manage preferences for personalized advertising in compliance with GDPR.

3. PRO Version and Purchases

Purchases made to unlock the PRO version are handled entirely by the Google Play Store or Apple App Store. We do not collect or store data related to credit cards or payment methods.

4. Backup and Data Transfer

The Backup function creates a file on your device. You decide if and where to share this file (e.g., email, personal cloud). FamLedger does not manage the automatic transfer of these files to its own servers.

5. User Rights

Since data is saved locally, you have full control over it. You can delete all data by clearing the app cache or using the "Delete all" function in the settings.

6. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, you can contact us at: famledger.app@gmail.com
